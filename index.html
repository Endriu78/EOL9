EOL Overtime Master Sheet
Encirc · The Park — End of Line
A tool for managing overtime allocation fairly across EOL teams — tracks shift history, enforces 60h weekly limits, and ensures every opportunity is distributed equitably.
What this app does
👤
Workers
Add and manage all EOL team members with skills, rates and team assignment.
📋
Allocation
Automatically allocate OT slots to the workers with fewest shifts first.
🎲
Fair Draw
When workers are tied on shift count, a random draw picks who gets the slot.
⏱
Shift Register
Log overtime shifts with automatic rate multipliers for nights, weekends and bank holidays.
⚠️
60h / 72h Rule
Checks weekly hours against the Working Time limit before any OT is approved.
🎓
Skills Matrix
Visual grid of who holds which skills — filter draws and allocation by skill.
📊
Reports
Monthly SAP grid, per-worker summaries, daily reports and shift summaries.
🗓
Schedule Import
Load the Excel rota to automatically include regular shift hours in the 60h calculation.
